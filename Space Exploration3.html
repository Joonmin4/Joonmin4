Apollo1(AS-204)

Apollo1 was meant to be the first of the Apollo missions. However,
it did not end well. Instead, it ended in a great tragedy.
A few seconds before launch, a spark in the control panels caused an
accidential fire in the command module. The astronauts tried to open
the door and get out, but a problem in the door caused it to not open.
All three astronauts, Virgil I."Gus" Grissom, Edward H. White II,
and Roger B. Chaffee, were killed in the fire.

Apollo7(SA-205)

Apollo7 was the first Apollo mission to successfully put humans
in space. The AS-204 flight 22 months ago was meant to be the first, but a
fire had burned up the command module. The Apollo 7 mission fufilled the
AS-204 missions purpose, becoming the first Apollo mission to orbit around
the Earth and to send humans to outer space with a Saturn 1B rocket.

Apollo8(SA-503)

Apollo8 was the second successful Apollo mission. It was also the
first Apollo mission to actually orbit the moon.They were able to observe
and take pictures of the surface of the moon.The SA-503 mission also the first
Apollo mission to ride on a Saturn V rocket.

Apollo9(SA-504)

The Apollo9 mission was the first Apollo mission to actually bring a lunar
module into space. The crew performed the first manned flight of a lunar
module, the first docking and extraction of a lunar module, another docking
of a lunar module, and two spacewalks(EVA). Its overall mission was to
test the lunar module in outer space.

Apollo10(SA-505)

Apollo10 was the second Apollo mission to orbit the moon. Its main objectives
were to act as a "dress rehearsal" for the first moon landing. The SA-505
missions call signs, Snoopy and Charlie Brown, became Apollo10s semi offical
mascot.

Apollo11(SA-506)

Apollo11 was the spaceflight that landed the first two humans, Neil
Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin, on the moon. When they had landed on the
surface of the moon, Armstrong announced, "The Eagle has landed."
Armstrong became the first to step on the moon;Aldrin joined him
about 20 minutes later. When Armstrong became the first human
to step on the moon, he said his famous words: "One small step
for man, one giant leap for mankind."

Apollo13(SA-508)

Apollo13 was the seventh manned Apollo mission and was meant to be the third
to land on the moon. However, 2 days after launch, the oxygen tank exploded
and forced the crew, James A. Lovell, John L. "Jack" Swigert, and Fred W. Haise,
to give up and go back.Fortunatly,nobody was hurt or injured from the incident.

Apollo15(SA-510)

Apollo15 was the eighth successful manned mission in the Apollo program. It was
also the first mission to use a Lunar Roving Vehicle(LRV). The LRV allowed
them to explore further than ever before. They stayed longer than before on
the moon,and were able to collect 77 kilograms(170lb) of lunar surface material.

Apollo17(SA-512)

Apollo17 was the last of the Apollo missions. It was also the first night launch
of a U.S. human spaceflight.
