SpaceShipTwo

SpaceShipTwo was built to have space tourism. It will be
carried up to its launch altitude by a White Knight
Two, before beeing released to fly with its own rocket
engine. The SpaceShipTwo company plans to have
passenger carrying service, with tickets of about
US $250,000.

SLS(Space Launch System)

The Space Launch System(SLS) rocket is part of NASAs
deep space exploration plans. SLS will be the most
powerful rocket ever built; even stronger than the
mighty Saturn V. SLS will replace the space shuttle
fleet, and, someday, take people to Mars.
